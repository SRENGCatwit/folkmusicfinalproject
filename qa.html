What is Folk Music?

Folk Music is a music genre that originated in the 1960s. It features instruments such as the fiddle, banjo, upright bass, and acoustic guitar. Passed down orally through generations within communities, folk music is found in diverse forms across the country...

What are some Genres of Folk Music?

Folk Music encompasses various genres, including Traditional, Bluegrass, Cajun, Zydeco, Delta Blues, Folk Rock, and Gospel.
