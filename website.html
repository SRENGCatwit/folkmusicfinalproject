Welcome to My Blog!

This website is dedicated to the History of American Folk Music, with a specific focus on Bluegrass. My journey began at a folk music venue located at Cambridge, Massachusetts called "Club Passim!" Through this platform, I aim to document my experience using videos, pictures, and text!

I've designed this website to be both creative and simple to navigate! You'll find it divided into different pages, each accessible via buttons at the top of the website. Clicking on a button will take you to a page dedicated to that section!
